# Complete Product Hunt Launch Guide

## üéØ What is Product Hunt?

Product Hunt is a platform where people discover new products. It's like "Hacker News for products." Getting featured on Product Hunt can drive thousands of visitors to your site in a single day.

**Key Facts:**
- Products are ranked by upvotes within a 24-hour period
- Top products get featured on the homepage
- Can drive 1,000-10,000+ visitors in one day
- Great for early traction and press coverage

## üö´ Why You Can't Post Your Own Product

**Product Hunt Rule**: Makers (the people who built the product) cannot post their own products. You need someone else to "hunt" it for you.

**Why?** This prevents self-promotion spam and keeps the community authentic.

## üë• Step 1: Find a Hunter

A "hunter" is someone with a Product Hunt account who will post your product for you.

### Where to Find Hunters:

**Option 1: Ask Friends/Network**
- Do you know anyone active on Product Hunt?
- Ask friends, colleagues, or people in your network
- Best if they have some Product Hunt karma (upvotes on their profile)

**Option 2: Product Hunt Community**
- Join Product Hunt's Slack community
- Post in #hunters channel asking for help
- Be genuine and explain your product

**Option 3: Twitter/X**
- Search for "Product Hunt hunter" or "PH hunter"
- Look for people who've hunted products before
- Reach out politely explaining your product

**Option 4: Indie Hackers / Reddit**
- Post on r/indiehackers or r/SideProject
- Ask if anyone would be willing to hunt your product
- Offer to return the favor

**Option 5: Hire a Hunter**
- Some people offer hunting services (usually $50-200)
- Search "Product Hunt hunter for hire"
- Make sure they have good karma and reviews

### What to Look for in a Hunter:
- ‚úÖ Active Product Hunt account
- ‚úÖ Some karma (upvotes they've received)
- ‚úÖ Has hunted products before (check their profile)
- ‚úÖ Responsive and reliable
- ‚úÖ Understands your product

## üìã Step 2: Prepare Your Product Hunt Listing

**You need to prepare everything BEFORE launch day.** Your hunter will need:

### Required Information:

**1. Product Name**
- Example: "Best Husband Ever"

**2. Tagline** (One sentence, max 60 characters)
- Example: "Daily actions to level up your marriage"

**3. Description** (The main product description)
- 2-4 paragraphs explaining what it is
- Include key features
- Keep it engaging and clear
- See template in LAUNCH_ANNOUNCEMENT_GUIDE.md

**4. Product URL**
- Your website: https://www.besthusbandever.com

**5. Product Logo** (512x512px)
- Square image
- PNG or JPG
- High quality
- Should look good at small sizes

**6. Product Screenshots** (At least 3, recommended 5-7)
- Show your product in action
- Desktop screenshots
- Mobile screenshots (if applicable)
- Different features/pages
- High quality, clear images
- Recommended size: 1200x800px or similar

**7. Product Video** (Optional but recommended)
- 30-60 second demo
- Shows your product in action
- Can be simple screen recording
- Upload to YouTube or Vimeo
- Include link in listing

**8. Topics/Tags**
- Choose 3-5 relevant topics
- Examples: "SaaS", "Web App", "Productivity", "Relationships", "Self-Improvement"

**9. Maker Comment** (Your story - you write this after launch)
- Your personal story
- Why you built it
- What makes it special
- Thank supporters
- See template in LAUNCH_ANNOUNCEMENT_GUIDE.md

## üìÖ Step 3: Choose Your Launch Date

**Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
- Avoid Monday (busy start of week)
- Avoid Friday (people check out)
- Avoid weekends (lower traffic)

**Best Time**: 12:01 AM PST (Pacific Standard Time)
- Product Hunt resets at midnight PST
- Posting at 12:01 AM gives you the full day
- More visibility = better ranking

**Why This Matters:**
- Products are ranked by upvotes within 24 hours
- Early posts get more visibility
- Full day = more time to collect upvotes

## üöÄ Step 4: Launch Day Process

### The Night Before (11:30 PM PST):

1. **Final Check**
   - [ ] All assets ready (logo, screenshots, video)
   - [ ] Description finalized
   - [ ] Hunter confirmed and ready
   - [ ] Your Product Hunt account created
   - [ ] You're ready to respond to comments

2. **Prepare Your Hunter**
   - Send them all assets
   - Send them the description
   - Confirm they're ready to post at 12:01 AM PST
   - Give them your Product Hunt username so you can claim it

### At 12:01 AM PST:

1. **Hunter Posts Your Product**
   - Hunter goes to Product Hunt
   - Clicks "Add a Product"
   - Fills in all the information you provided
   - Submits at 12:01 AM PST

2. **You Claim Your Product**
   - As soon as it's posted, go to your product page
   - Click "Claim" or "I made this"
   - Product Hunt will verify you're the maker
   - You're now the "maker" and can add your maker comment

3. **Add Your Maker Comment**
   - Write your personal story
   - Thank early supporters
   - Engage with the community
   - This is your chance to connect with people

### Throughout Launch Day:

1. **Share Everywhere**
   - Share on Twitter/X
   - Share on LinkedIn
   - Share in relevant communities
   - Email your network
   - Ask friends to upvote

2. **Engage Actively**
   - Respond to every comment (within first hour especially)
   - Answer questions
   - Thank supporters
   - Be genuine and helpful

3. **Monitor Ranking**
   - Check your position throughout the day
   - Top 5 = featured on homepage (huge visibility)
   - Top 20 = great success
   - Top 50 = good success

4. **Stay Active**
   - Keep responding to comments
   - Share updates
   - Engage with other products too (community matters)

## üí° Pro Tips for Success

### Before Launch:

1. **Build an Email List**
   - Collect emails before launch
   - Email them on launch day asking for support
   - These early upvotes are crucial

2. **Prepare Your Network**
   - Tell friends/family about launch day
   - Ask them to upvote and comment
   - Early momentum is key

3. **Create Great Assets**
   - Screenshots should be clear and compelling
   - Logo should be professional
   - Video (if you have one) should be engaging

4. **Write Compelling Copy**
   - Clear, engaging description
   - Highlight what makes you different
   - Include social proof if you have it

### During Launch:

1. **Respond Quickly**
   - First hour is critical
   - Respond to comments within minutes
   - This boosts visibility

2. **Be Genuine**
   - Don't be salesy
   - Share your real story
   - Connect with people

3. **Engage with Community**
   - Upvote other products
   - Comment on other products
   - Product Hunt values community participation

4. **Share Strategically**
   - Don't spam
   - Share in relevant communities
   - Personal messages > public posts

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:

1. **Launching Too Early**
   - Make sure your product is ready
   - Test everything first
   - Broken products get downvoted

2. **Ignoring Comments**
   - Respond to everyone
   - Engage actively
   - People notice when makers are responsive

3. **Being Salesy**
   - Don't just promote
   - Share your story
   - Be helpful and genuine

4. **Not Preparing**
   - Have everything ready
   - Don't scramble on launch day
   - Preparation = success

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I post my own product?**
A: No, you need a hunter. But you can claim it as the maker once it's posted.

**Q: What if I can't find a hunter?**
A: Try Product Hunt Slack, Twitter, or Reddit. You can also hire one.

**Q: What if I miss 12:01 AM PST?**
A: Post as early as possible. Earlier is better, but any time on launch day works.

**Q: Can I launch multiple times?**
A: No, you can only launch once. Make it count!

**Q: What if I get negative comments?**
A: Respond professionally. Address concerns. Use feedback to improve.

**Q: How long does the ranking last?**
A: Rankings are for that day only. After 24 hours, it moves to "Made the Hunt" section.

**Q: Do I need a video?**
A: No, but it helps. Screenshots are required, video is optional but recommended.
