## ðŸ“± 1. Reddit Marketing (HIGHEST PRIORITY - FREE & EFFECTIVE)

### Best Subreddits to Engage:
- **r/relationship_advice** - Share helpful tips (don't self-promote, just help)
- **r/Marriage** - Engage in discussions, offer genuine advice
- **r/AskMen** - Answer relationship questions authentically
- **r/daddit** - Great for dads wanting to be better partners
- **r/selfimprovement** - Share your journey/insights
- **r/DecidingToBeBetter** - Perfect fit for your audience

### Reddit Strategy:
1. **Be Genuinely Helpful First**
   - Answer questions with real advice
   - Share relationship insights (not your product)
   - Build credibility over 2-3 months

2. **Soft Promotion (After Building Trust)**
   - When relevant, mention: "I built a tool that helps with this..."
   - Share success stories (anonymized)
   - Offer free access to Redditors who DM you

3. **Create Value Posts**
   - "10 Small Things I Did That Saved My Marriage"
   - "Daily Habits That Transformed My Relationship"
   - Share actionable tips (not just promoting your app)

4. **Engage in Comments**
   - Don't just post - comment on others' posts
   - Build relationships with active community members
   - Your profile can link to your site

### Reddit Rules:
- âœ… Read each subreddit's rules first
- âœ… No direct links in posts (usually)
- âœ… Be helpful, not salesy
- âœ… Engage authentically
- âŒ Don't spam or self-promote aggressively
