## üé® Design Quick Reference

### Colors
- **Primary Background:** #0f172a (Dark slate)
- **Primary Text:** #f1f5f9 (Light slate)
- **Accent:** #f97316 (Warm orange)
- **Secondary Accent:** #3b82f6 (Soft blue)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, sans-serif (Inter, Montserrat, or system font)
- **Body:** Regular weight, readable sans-serif
- **Keep it simple and clean**

### Image Sizes
- **Instagram Post:** 1080x1080px (square)
- **Instagram Story:** 1080x1920px (vertical)
- **Facebook Post:** 1200x630px (landscape) or 1080x1080px (square)
- **Facebook Cover:** 1200x630px
- **Profile Pictures:** 400x400px (Instagram), 180x180px (Facebook)

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Start Simple** - Don't overthink the first posts. Just start.
2. **Be Consistent** - Post regularly (daily on Instagram, 3-5x/week on Facebook)
3. **Engage Authentically** - Respond to comments, engage with others
4. **Use Hashtags** - 10-15 relevant hashtags per Instagram post
5. **Tell Stories** - People connect with real stories, not corporate speak
6. **Show Behind the Scenes** - Build connection and authenticity
7. **Ask Questions** - Get engagement with questions in captions
8. **Cross-Promote** - Link Instagram and Facebook accounts
