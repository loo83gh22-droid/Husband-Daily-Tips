## ðŸŽ¨ Design Guidelines

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, sans-serif (Inter, Montserrat, or similar)
- **Body:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Accent:** Can use serif for quotes/pullouts

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Dark slate (#0f172a)
- **Text:** Light slate (#f1f5f9)
- **Accent:** Warm orange/amber (#f97316, #fb923c) for highlights
- **Secondary Accent:** Soft blue (#3b82f6) for trust/calm

### Imagery Style
- **Photography:** Real, authentic moments (not stock photos)
- **Illustrations:** Simple, clean, minimal
- **Icons:** Modern, flat design
- **Avoid:** Overly corporate, cheesy, or generic stock photos

### Tone in Visuals
- Warm and approachable
- Not preachy or judgmental
- Masculine but not macho
- Professional but relatable
- Authentic and real
