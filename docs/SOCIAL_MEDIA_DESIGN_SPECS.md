## ğŸ¨ Typography Hierarchy

- Font: Inter Bold or Montserrat Bold
- Size: 48-72px (depending on context)
- Color: #f1f5f9 or #f97316
- Letter spacing: -1 to -2px

- Font: Inter SemiBold or Montserrat SemiBold
- Size: 32-42px

- Font: Inter Regular or Montserrat Regular

- Line height: 1.4-1.6

### Small Text/Captions:

- Size: 18-20px
