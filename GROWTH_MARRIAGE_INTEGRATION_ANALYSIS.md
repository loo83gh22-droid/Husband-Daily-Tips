## üí° Specific Action Ideas from Growth Marriage Philosophy

### **Roommate Syndrome Recovery Actions:**
1. "Have a 20-minute conversation with no phones or distractions"
2. "Ask your partner about their day and listen without offering solutions"
3. "Share one thing you appreciate about your partner today"
4. "Plan a surprise that shows you know your partner well"
5. "Have a 'state of the union' conversation about your relationship"
6. "Do something together that you both used to enjoy"
7. "Ask your partner: 'What can I do to make you feel more loved?'"

1. "Create a 'love map' - ask 20 questions about your partner's inner world"
2. "Practice the 5:1 ratio - make 5 positive comments before any criticism"
3. "Have a weekly 'date night' conversation (not just activities)"
4. "Share one thing you're grateful for about your partner"
5. "Practice 'turning toward' - respond positively to your partner's bids for attention"

### **Growth-Focused Actions:**
1. "Identify one area where you want to grow as a partner"
2. "Ask your partner: 'What's one thing I could do better?'"
3. "Read one relationship book chapter together this week"
4. "Attend a relationship workshop or event together"
5. "Set one relationship goal for the next 30 days"
