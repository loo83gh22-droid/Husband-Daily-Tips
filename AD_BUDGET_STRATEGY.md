## üí∞ **What $2/Day Gets You**

### Realistic Expectations
- **Daily spend**: $2/day = $60/month = $720/year
- **Estimated clicks**: 20-50 clicks/day (depending on CPC of $0.04-$0.10)
- **Estimated signups**: 1-5 signups/day (assuming 2-10% conversion rate)
- **Monthly signups**: ~30-150 new users/month

### The Math
- **Average CPC** (Cost Per Click) on Facebook/Instagram: $0.50-$2.00 for relationship/self-help
- **Your budget**: $2/day √∑ $0.50 CPC = **4 clicks/day minimum**
- **Your budget**: $2/day √∑ $2.00 CPC = **1 click/day maximum**

**Reality check**: At $2/day, you're looking at **4-10 clicks per day** on average, which means **0-2 signups per day** if your conversion rate is 10-20%.

## ‚ö†Ô∏è **What You MUST Have Ready First**

### 1. **Analytics & Tracking** (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Google Analytics set up and working
- [ ] Facebook Pixel installed (for conversion tracking)
- [ ] UTM parameters on your ad links
- [ ] Conversion tracking for signups
- [ ] Ability to see which ads lead to signups

**Why**: Without tracking, you're spending blind. You won't know if ads are working.

### 2. **Landing Page Optimization**
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Strong call-to-action
- [ ] Mobile-optimized (most ad traffic is mobile)
- [ ] Fast load times (<3 seconds)
- [ ] No broken links or errors

**Why**: If your landing page doesn't convert, you're wasting ad spend.

### 3. **Support System Ready**
- [ ] Support email set up
- [ ] FAQ page ready
- [ ] Ability to respond to user questions quickly
- [ ] Process for handling bugs/issues

**Why**: New users will have questions. Be ready to help.

### 4. **App Stability**
- [ ] Core features working
- [ ] Signup flow tested
- [ ] Payment flow tested (if applicable)
- [ ] No critical bugs

**Why**: First impressions matter. Broken features = lost users.

### 5. **Conversion Funnel Tested**
- [ ] Test: Ad click ‚Üí Landing page ‚Üí Signup ‚Üí First action
- [ ] Verify tracking works end-to-end
- [ ] Check that users can actually complete signup

**Why**: You need to know your funnel works before spending money.

## üìä **What to Track**

### Key Metrics
1. **Cost Per Click (CPC)**: How much each click costs
2. **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: % of people who click your ad
3. **Cost Per Signup**: How much each signup costs
4. **Signup Rate**: % of visitors who sign up
5. **Trial-to-Paid Conversion**: % who convert to paid
6. **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: Revenue √∑ Ad spend

### Goals
- **CPC**: Aim for <$1.00
- **CTR**: Aim for >1% (good), >2% (great)
- **Cost Per Signup**: Aim for <$5-10
- **Signup Rate**: Aim for >5%

## üö® **Risks & How to Mitigate**

### Risk 1: Wasting Money on Wrong Audience
**Mitigation:**
- Start with very specific targeting
- Test small audiences first
- Use Facebook's interest targeting (relationship, marriage, self-improvement)
- Exclude people who already visited your site (retargeting)

### Risk 2: Landing Page Doesn't Convert
**Mitigation:**
- Test landing page before running ads
- Use A/B testing if possible
- Monitor bounce rate (should be <60%)
- Track time on page (should be >30 seconds)

### Risk 3: Not Enough Data to Learn
**Mitigation:**
- Run ads for at least 7-14 days before making decisions
- Don't change too many things at once
- Track everything
- Be patient

### Risk 4: App Not Ready for Users
**Mitigation:**
- Complete pre-launch checklist
- Test everything yourself first
- Have support ready
- Monitor error logs

## üí° **Best Practices for $2/Day Budget**

### 1. **Start Narrow, Then Expand**
- Target very specific audience first
- Once you find what works, expand
- Example: Start with "Married men, 30-45, interested in relationships"

### 2. **Test One Thing at a Time**
- Test ad copy first
- Then test images
- Then test audiences
- Don't change everything at once

### 3. **Use Facebook's Learning Phase**
- Facebook needs 50 conversions to optimize
- At $2/day, this could take weeks
- Be patient during learning phase

### 4. **Focus on Quality Over Quantity**
- Better to get 1 good signup than 10 bad ones
- Target people who are likely to convert
- Use lookalike audiences once you have data

### 5. **Set Up Retargeting**
- Retarget people who visited but didn't sign up
- Usually cheaper and converts better
- Can start with $1/day for retargeting

## üì± **Building Social Media Presence While Running Ads**

### Why This Matters
Having an active social media presence helps with ads because:
- **Social proof**: People see you're active and legitimate
- **Lower ad costs**: Facebook rewards accounts with engagement
- **Better targeting**: You can retarget people who engaged with your posts
- **Trust building**: Consistent posting shows you're serious

### Strategy: Organic + Paid Together

**Week 1-2: Build Foundation (Before Ads)**
- Post daily for 1-2 weeks
- Build to 10-20 posts minimum
- Get some initial followers (friends, family, early supporters)
- Establish your voice and content style

**Week 3+: Run Ads + Continue Posting**
- Keep posting daily (organic)
- Start $2-5/day ads
- Use your best organic posts as ad creative
- Retarget people who engaged with organic posts

### Content Mix for Daily Posts

**80% Value, 20% Promotion:**
- **Monday**: Relationship tip/insight
- **Tuesday**: Daily action example
- **Wednesday**: Behind the scenes/building story
- **Thursday**: User question/FAQ
- **Friday**: Quick win/tip
- **Saturday**: Community highlight/testimonial
- **Sunday**: Weekly reflection/challenge

**Promotional posts (20%):**
- Once or twice per week
- Soft sell: "If this resonates, check us out"
- Link to free trial

### How Organic Helps Ads

1. **Lower Costs**: Accounts with engagement get better ad rates
2. **Better Targeting**: Can create "Engaged Audience" custom audiences
3. **Social Proof**: People see you're active before clicking ad
4. **Retargeting**: Retarget people who liked/commented on posts
5. **Lookalike Audiences**: Once you have engaged followers, create lookalikes

### Quick Wins for Building Presence

**Before Running Ads:**
- [ ] Post 10-15 times (daily for 2 weeks)
- [ ] Get 50+ followers (friends, family, early users)
- [ ] Get some engagement (likes, comments)
- [ ] Set up Instagram highlights
- [ ] Complete your profile (bio, cover photo, etc.)

**While Running Ads:**
- [ ] Continue daily posting
- [ ] Boost your best-performing organic posts ($1-2/day)
- [ ] Use organic posts as ad creative
- [ ] Retarget post engagers

### Timeline Recommendation

**Week 1-2: Organic Only**
- Post daily
- Build followers
- Establish presence
- **No ads yet**

**Week 3: Start Small**
- Continue daily posting
- Start $2/day ads
- Test messaging
- Learn what works

**Week 4+: Scale**
- Double down on what works
- Increase ad budget if profitable
- Use organic insights to improve ads
